Wellroute was established in 2013 as an aggregation of related businesses that provide intermediary services for corporate, institutional and international clients.

We have a trusted and effective way that delivers rest of mind to our clients backed by a qualified strategy team and our on-the-go operational system, supported by our technical partners, that guarantees a robust customer service experience.

We at Wellroute have a dogged resolution to provide our varied clientele with a wide range of highly customizable services with the aim of satisfying their individual tastes and preferences.

We will achieve this through our strategic partnership and affiliations which gives us the unique edge that we possess to cut the best deal for our clientele at all levels, design a cashback guarantee in-route hand holding to the market for our small and medium scale businesses, and an effective project management.

We are privileged through our affiliates to have provided services to Public and Private Organizations, Paramilitary, and Government Agencies such as Federal Road Safety Corps (FRSC), Standards Organization of Nigeria (SON), Nigeria National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC), Petroleum and Natural Gas Senior Staff Association of Nigeria (PENGASSAN), amongst others.

OUR VISION

To become the foremost independent Business Development, Entrepreneurial Enhancement, and Nation Building Company, popularly known for delivering an effective, excellent and first rate services in record time to individual, corporate, government and retail markets in Sub­Saharan Africa.

OUR MISSION

Wellroute is strategically positioned to determine the condition of play at all levels as Business Development, and Nation Building experts.

Wellroute will help Businesses, Corporations, Government Establishments and Foreign Agencies to effectively situate their vision, interpret their Mission Statements, and achieve their set out goals and objectives.

Annually, 50 to 5,000 Small and Medium Scale Enterprises will be provided in­route to the market and stabilized.

Projects will be delivered within budget and on time with an unequaled attention to details
